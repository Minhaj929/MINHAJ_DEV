Meet Marquita

As an artist, vocal coach and mentor, I have years of experience to draw from and give you. I’m passionate about
sharing the tools of the trade, to help you have success with your singing journey. There are
different avenues we can take together. Whether it’s private lessons, group coaching, or
specialized courses, there will be one that fits you and your vocal needs.
Let me help you be the best singer you can be!
