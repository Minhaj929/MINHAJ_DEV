Meet Marquita

As an artist, vocal coach and mentor, I have years of experience to draw from and give you. I’m passionate about
sharing the tools of the trade, to help you have success with your singing journey. There are
different avenues we can take together. Whether it’s private lessons, group coaching, or
specialized courses, there will be one that fits you and your vocal needs.
Let me help you be the best singer you can be!

Singing for a Lifetime

There’s no greater feeling than soaring through your song and knowing you nailed it. I know the feeling, and when you join Singing for a Lifetime and train with us, you’ll get to know that feeling too!
Please note that this training and exercises are not for the male voice—sorry gents. The exercises and their range fit the female voices perfectly. The vocal exercise and training will challenge you, so get ready for great vocal workouts each week!
